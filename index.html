Strength Classes - L/N/R Variants

Critical Correction

Strength classes are completely separate from cement types.

L (Low early strength): Only available for CEM III cements

N (Normal early strength): Available for all cement types

R (Rapid early strength): Available for all except CEM III-L combinations

Chemical requirements are determined by cement TYPE, not strength class.
